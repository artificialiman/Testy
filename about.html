Our Mission

To develop every student — academically, athletically, creatively, and in character — so that they leave Tendercare bigger than when they arrived.

Tendercare Comprehensive College was founded on a single conviction: that a child's education should leave nothing on the table. Not academic potential. Not athletic ability. Not creative expression. Not moral formation.

Twenty-five years later, that conviction has produced generations of graduates who carry something the world can feel.

25 Years

How we got here.

Founded · 2000

Tendercare Comprehensive College opens its doors with a founding class and an unwavering belief in total education.

Early Growth · 2005

Secondary division established. The school becomes all-through — serving students from primary through to final year.

Recognition · 2012

First cohort of external competition entrants. Science fair, debate, and mathematics Olympiad become annual fixtures.

1st Interhouse · 2023

The first Interhouse Sports Festival launches — an ambitious declaration of what Tendercare could do when it performed publicly.

3rd Interhouse · 2025

The festival becomes a tradition. A proof. A statement of capacity sent to the world: if we can do this in sport, we can do it in anything.
